What is Ignite?

Many successful engineering students have not always been toppers right from school level, but they have all shared the common characteristic of being able to think differently from the status quo, and be able to approach any question in the right manner, resting upon their understanding of the concepts rather than their ability to recollect and remember formulae.

"Ignite" is a test whose ultimate objective is to develop the right way of thinking and help teach the students a better way of approaching any type of problem posed. The test aims to do so by giving students ample opportunities to develop their thinking abilities and hone their intelligence, compared to their prior knowledge.

Scientific and highly analytical techniques are employed right from the selection of questions to the preparation of the objective answers, and a dedicated team of IITians specially have come together to work on the analysis of a student’s approach to a question, and brainstorm ways to inculcate the right way to probe a question and arrive at the correct answer.

Test Details and Syllabus

Obective exam based on OMR, The exam tests understanding of Physics, Mathematics and Logical Reasoning. Students from Class 6 to 10 can attend the test. Basically the exam will be testing students on their Maths and Physics knowledge of their respective class levels. The students are advised to be prepared on basic concepts of these subjects.

Aim

The test aims to see how well students grasp concepts and reasoning as compared to how well they recall previously heard formulae, and it does so by introducing questions with relatively unheard concepts explained with an introduction, and lets the students arrive at the conclusion to the question based solely on their understanding ability and not on prior knowledge.

Date and Prize details

The exam date will be on the 27th of October, and the venue will be at your own school. Examination fee will be 100 Rs. and can be paid at School office. Each district topper will be given a tablet computer that has been pre-loaded with educational software and applications.

About Us

Who are we? What is our objective?

A group of enterprising IITians, inspired by the philosophy of "Giving Back to the Society" and “Focussing on the Grassroots” who have taken it into their stride to stop thoughtless rote-learning, which is threatening the prospects of fostering a conceptual scientific fraternity in India.

SSubjective and objective memorizations are prevalent among students. Previously, it used to be learning of subjective questions, but now the students have moved on to learning the answers to objective questions through repetition and continuous re-reading. They are focusing on remembering the cases of formulae application rather than concentrating upon the origin and application of the concepts involved.

IIgnite gives priority to formatting questions so that they test conceptual understanding of the studentsrather than simple memorization, similar to the general engineering entrance exams currently prevalent.
